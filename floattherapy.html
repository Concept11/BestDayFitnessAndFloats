THE TANK

It looks like a large, egg shaped bath tub. With the lid closed (optional), music and lights off (also optional), all external distractions, such as sights, sounds, tactile sensation and gravity are blocked.

TEMPERATURE

The salt water is heated to skin temperature (93.5 degrees Fahrenheit). Once you are settled into the tank it is difficult to tell which parts of your body are in the water and which are not. This gives a feeling of floating in mid air.

EPSOM SALT

With 1000 pounds of Epsom salts in the tank you will be extremely buoyant. You will float without effort and feel almost completely weightless allowing every muscle in your body to fully relax.

WHY

This will most lilkely result in you experiencing the most relaxing time of your life. Without all that external stimulation your mind is free to think, dream, create & process without distraction. With the outside world gone, amazing things can happen.
